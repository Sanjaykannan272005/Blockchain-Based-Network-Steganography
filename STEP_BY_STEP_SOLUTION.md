## 📋 The 5 Steps

### ✅ STEP 1: Configure Sender
- Enter your wallet address
- Default provided
- Click "Next"

### ✅ STEP 2: Configure Receiver
- Choose: GUI Receiver (same page) or Network Receiver (different computer)
- If network: enter IP address
- Click "Next"

### ✅ STEP 3: Select Features
- **Recommended:** Blockchain Keys (pre-selected)
- **Additional:** Authentication, Reputation, Forensics
- **Advanced:** 6 more features (click "Show More")
- Click "Next"

### ✅ STEP 4: Write Message
- Type your secret message
- Click "Next"

### ✅ STEP 5: Review & Send
- See summary of all settings
- Click "🚀 SEND MESSAGE"
- **Result appears automatically!**
